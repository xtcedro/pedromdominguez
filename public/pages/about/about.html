👋 Hola, I’m Pedro M. Dominguez

🌵 My Story

I was born in Ciudad Juárez, México 🇲🇽 and grew up in Praxedis G. Guerrero before my family brought me to Oklahoma City. Ever since, I’ve been driven by a single goal: to give my community and local businesses the power to own their digital real estate — from database to domain.

🧩 Why I Built DenoGenesis

Big Tech has turned the web into a maze of black-box systems, hidden costs, and vendor lock-ins. I see that as modern techno-feudalism — and I’m not here for it. I built DenoGenesis to flip that script. It’s a sovereign-first meta framework that makes local-first, audit-friendly development possible for anyone — whether you’re an entrepreneur, a small business owner, or a community org.

I blend my corridos roots, my love for Linux, and my cinematic design sense into every project. From custom boot sequences to scroll-triggered effects, every site I build is transparent, human, and yours to own — forever. No black-box AI. No hidden middlemen. Just open, sovereign code.
